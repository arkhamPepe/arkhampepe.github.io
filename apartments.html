Estepona Paradise

This grand white summer house is situated solitarily and in the center on the island in tranquil and naturaally beautiful surroundings. Thankfully, due to the amazing location på on a small slope one will experience a view out of the ordinary from all of the estate. The house itself is an ancient mallorcian kingdom, where one blends good comfort with old traditions. The great swimming pool lies directly in front of the house and the terraces along with being surrounded by a neatly trimmed and well taken cared of garden. Other upper planes are not decorated for living, but still offers a big room with a ping pong table. The small village of Petra sure is a nice excursion.

Benalmadema Palace

Benalmadena Palace is situated nearby the beach of Benalmadena, 4 km off the nearest golf course and 300 m away from bars, shops and restaurants. At the hotel there is a reception kept in check 24h every day of the week, bar, buffet restaurant, pool, sun terrace, kid's pool, mini club, indoors pool, gym, sauna, Jacuzzi, pub, mini grocery store, garage, entertainment, discotheque, open laundry and a conference room.

All apartments are equiped with bathroom/shower, pentry, telephone, satelite-TV, balcony/terrace and air conditioning/central heating. Change of towels: 3 times/week. Change of bedsheets: 2 times/week.
